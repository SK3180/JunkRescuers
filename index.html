Reward System

How it Works ?

The Reward system will work on the basis of your waste type and weight of your waste.

Once the waste gets collected, Points will be given to consumer depending on the weight of waste as shown in table.

The points gets converted into voulchers which can be used at various online shopping websites like flipkart, Amazon, Mintra etc.
